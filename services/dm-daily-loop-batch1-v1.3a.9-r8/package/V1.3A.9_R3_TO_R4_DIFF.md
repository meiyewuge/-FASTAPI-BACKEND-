- Provider由`PlatformRecoveryProvider.from_env(clock=clock)`在构造时创建
- 调用方无法注入自建Provider

- Truth Gate Test 16: 使用`kr.encrypt_field()` + `kr.decrypt_field()` + `assertRaises(InvalidTag)`

### P0-4: MutationSpec expected_hits
- 每项mutation显式声明expected_hits
- m10: disabled条件移除 (verifier mix)
- m11: expiry判断移除
